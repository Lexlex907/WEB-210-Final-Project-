How to Place an Order

At OSS Nails, we offer custom luxury press-on nails with Christian-inspired designs. Every set is made to order with custom sizes and designs available!

Order Process

  • Send us an email at: osscontactus.onestopshop@gmail.com
  • In the email, please provide:
    • Your full name
    • The product name or custom design you're interested in
    • Your preferred nail size (if known)
    • Any additional design requests
  • We will respond with a confirmation and payment instructions.
  • Once payment is received, we will begin creating your custom order.
